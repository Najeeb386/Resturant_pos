Restaurant / Fast Food Management System
Complete Requirements Document + Software Requirement Specification (SRS)
1. Project Overview
Project Name

Smart Restaurant & Fast Food Management System

Project Type

Web-Based SaaS Application (Later Expandable to Android APK / Mobile App)

Purpose

The purpose of this system is to fully digitize and automate restaurant and fast-food operations including:

POS Billing
Table Management
Kitchen Operations
Delivery & Takeaway Orders
Inventory & Stock Management
Expense & Profit Tracking
Reports & Analytics
Staff Management
Customer Management

The system should support:

Small Restaurants
Cafes
Fast Food Shops
Multi-Branch Restaurants
Cloud Kitchens
2. Main Objectives

The system should help restaurant owners to:

Reduce manual work
Track sales in real time
Manage kitchen efficiently
Avoid stock shortages
Improve billing speed
Monitor profits & expenses
Manage dine-in, takeaway & delivery
Generate business reports instantly
Handle multiple branches

12. Software Requirement Specification (SRS)
12.1 Introduction

The Restaurant Management System is a complete ERP-style web application developed to automate restaurant operations including ordering, billing, inventory, kitchen workflow, reporting, and customer management.

12.2 System Scope

The system shall:

Manage dine-in and takeaway operations
Track inventory automatically
Generate financial reports
Manage staff and kitchen
Support multi-branch operations
